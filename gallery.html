Orthographic projection, viewer at +X equatorial
axis tilted 6° above the equatorial plane.
  Globe radius R   416 px  (at 900 px wide)
  Centre x         50% of canvas
  Centre y         ~90% of canvas height

  → Lower ~44% of sphere is cropped by frame

  Interior fill    #EDE5CF  (barely-warm beige)
  Grid lines       #C8B690, 0.85 px stroke
  Outline circle   #C2AF88, 0.9 px stroke

LATITUDE PARALLELS
Every 10° from −80° to +80° (17 drawn, ~9 visible).
Each is a horizontal ellipse:
  centre_y = cy − R·sin(lat)·cos(6°)
  rx = R·cos(lat)
  ry = R·cos(lat)·sin(6°)

LONGITUDE MERIDIANS
Every 18° — 20 half-arcs = 10 great circles.
Parametric, 1° increments from S-pole → N-pole.
  sx = cx + R · cos(lat)·sin(lon)
  sy = cy − R · [sin(lat)·cos(e) − cos(lat)·cos(lon)·sin(e)]
