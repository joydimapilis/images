Custom Models

Prompt 
Create a minimal, flat UI panel on a warm cream background that
displays three vertically-stacked AI model-type selector cards.

── Canvas & Frame ──────────────────────────────────────────────
• Background color: #F2EBD9 (warm parchment / off-white cream)
• The entire composition sits inside an invisible bounding box
  ~780 px wide. At each corner of this bounding box place a small
  filled square (14 × 14 px, border-radius 2 px) as anchor/corner
  markers:
    top-left     → #E04040  (coral red)
    top-right    → #E87A20  (burnt orange)
    bottom-left  → #6B2DB8  (violet / purple)
    bottom-right → #D49020  (amber / gold)

── Card Stack ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
Three horizontally-wide cards stacked with 20 px vertical gap.
All cards share:
  • Border-radius: 6 px
  • Border: 1.5 px solid #DDD5C0
  • Background: #F5EFE2 (very light warm beige)
  • Content: centered icon + label side-by-side, icon 14 px to
    the left of text
  • Icon color: #5A5347 (warm dark gray)
  • Label color: #4A4440 (deep warm gray)
  • Font: Inter (or system sans-serif), semi-bold (600)

Card 1 — "Transcription"
  • Padding: 28 px top/bottom, 40 px left/right
  • Icon: double-document / clipboard (outline stroke 1.7)
  • Label: 22 px / 600 weight

Card 2 — "Large language model"  [FEATURED / middle]
  • Padding: 36 px top/bottom, 40 px left/right  (taller than others)
  • Background: #EDE4CC  (slightly darker warm beige — stands out)
  • Border: 1.5 px solid #D8CDB4
  • Icon: brain / neural outline (outline stroke 1.7), 32 px
  • Label: 26 px / 600 weight  (slightly larger text)

Card 3 — "Text-to-speech"
  • Same spec as Card 1
  • Icon: microphone outline (outline stroke 1.7)
  • Label: 22 px / 600 weight

── Typography ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
Font family: Inter, Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif
Weights used: 600 (semi-bold) only
Letter-spacing: -0.01 em for a tight, modern feel
No subtext, captions, or secondary labels anywhere.

── Visual Style ────────────────────────────────────────────────
Flat design — no drop shadows, no gradients, no blur effects.
Warm neutral monochromatic palette with tiny accent corner dots.
The overall feel is an infographic-style "model type" picker,
clean and minimal, suitable for a SaaS product marketing section.

── Interactions (optional) ─────────────────────────────────────
On card hover: lighten border to #C8BFA8, raise background to
#EAE0CE. Transition: 150 ms ease. No scale or shadow transforms.

Global Voice

Prompt 
Create a minimal, flat illustration of a wireframe globe on a warm
cream background, partially cropped at the bottom so only the top
~56% of the sphere is visible.

── Canvas ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Background colour: #F2EBD9 (warm parchment / off-white cream)
• Canvas aspect ratio: ~1.84 : 1  (e.g. 900 × 490 px)
• overflow: hidden on the container

── Globe ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• The globe is centered horizontally and positioned so its top arc
  ("North Pole") appears ~4–6% from the top of the canvas.
• The globe center is placed ~88–90% down the canvas height, causing
  the lower ~44% of the sphere to be cropped by the frame.
• Globe interior fill:   #EDE5CF  (warm beige, barely darker than bg)
• Grid line colour:      #C8B690  (warm tan / aged parchment tone)
• Grid line weight:      0.85 px
• Globe outline circle:  #C2AF88, 0.9 px stroke
• Projection: orthographic, viewing from the positive-X equatorial
  direction, tilted ~6° above the equatorial plane.

── Globe Grid ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Latitude parallels every 10° from −80° to +80° (17 lines drawn;
  ~9 visible within the canvas).
  Each parallel is a true horizontal ellipse:
    centre_y = globe_cy − R · sin(lat) · cos(elev)
    rx = R · cos(lat)
    ry = R · cos(lat) · sin(elev)
• Longitude meridians every 18° (20 half-arcs = 10 great circles).
  Drawn parametrically from S-pole to N-pole, 1° increments.

── Anchor Squares ──────────────────────────────────────────────
Six small filled squares (13×13 px, border-radius 2px) placed at
specific reference positions on the composition:

  coral red    #E04040  — top-center-left  (~35%, 27%)
  burnt orange #E87A20  — top-center-right (~58%, 27%)
  amber/gold   #D4A018  — mid-left         (~23%, 45%)
  violet       #7B2FBE  — mid-right        (~71%, 45%)
  electric blue #2B6DE8 — lower-left      (~24%, 91%)
  emerald green #1BA830 — lower-right     (~76%, 91%)

── Brand / Logo Mark ───────────────────────────────────────────
• A single bold, condensed black letterform ("b" or brand glyph)
  centered horizontally at approx. (50%, 61%) of the canvas.
• Colour: #0e0e0e (near-black)
• Font style: Impact or Arial Black, 900 weight, ~66px, line-height 0.88
• No shadow, no outline — plain solid fill on the warm globe surface.

── Colour Palette ──────────────────────────────────────────────
Page background   #F2EBD9   Globe fill       #EDE5CF
Grid lines        #C8B690   Globe outline    #C2AF88
Logo mark         #0E0E0E   Anchor 1 (red)   #E04040
Anchor 2 (orange) #E87A20   Anchor 3 (gold)  #D4A018
Anchor 4 (violet) #7B2FBE   Anchor 5 (blue)  #2B6DE8
Anchor 6 (green)  #1BA830

── Visual Style ────────────────────────────────────────────────
Flat / minimal illustration — zero drop shadows, zero gradients.
Warm monochromatic palette with small multi-coloured accent dots.
Globe evokes "global reach" or "international voice" concept.
The cropped bottom creates a sense of scale and grandeur.

Workflow Diagram

Prompt 
Create a minimalist flat editorial UI illustration on a 2516 × 1364 px
artboard. The background is a warm ivory/cream color (#fbf7ea) with a
very thin dark outer border (#25241f) and a subtle 6 px viewport corner
radius.

── Card Stack ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
Stack four large horizontal process cards, each 1222 px wide and
260 px high, aligned at x=631 px. Place them at y=226, 538, 851,
and 1163 px so the lowest card is partially cropped by the bottom
edge. Cards use:
  • Fill: #fbf8ec (warm off-white)
  • Border: 4 px solid #c4c0aa
  • Border-radius: 5 px
  • Shadow: very soft downward

── Rail & Pipes ────────────────────────────────────────────────
Behind the cards, add a vertical pale beige rail (#f1ecdc)
at x=735 px, y=483 px, width 1017 px, height 783 px.

Add thick routed connector lines:
  Blue  #205dac — left side, curves top-left radius 118 px, 6 px stroke
  Amber #f2a406 — drops from top, curves bottom-right corner 118 px
  Red   #b50e17 — far right, curves bottom-left with 116 px radius
  Olive #b58c2d — lower left, partial arc emerging from edge

── Swatches (inside each card) ─────────────────────────────────
Four small squares (50×50 px, gap 18 px) near upper-left of each
card. Default: muted beige (#cec8b0 / #bcb7a0). One highlighted:
  Card 1 → 2nd swatch: blue  #205dac (4 px radius)
  Card 2 → 4th swatch: amber #f2a406
  Card 3 → 1st swatch: red   #b50e17
  Card 4 → 3rd swatch: blue  #205dac
Beige swatches are perfectly square; colored swatches have 4 px radius.

Each card has a muted beige bar (247×50 px) at lower-right,
right 36 px, bottom 43 px. Top card bar at top 163 px instead.

── Brand Mark ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
On card 2, right side: "Bland®" wordmark
  Font: Arial/Helvetica bold grotesk, 800 weight, 53 px
  Color: #7d776c, tracking -2.5 px, superscript ® at 18 px

── Visual Style ────────────────────────────────────────────────
No gradients, no glass, no material shadows. Flat, geometric,
diagrammatic. Oversized negative space, precision alignment, muted
neutral palette, small saturated accent markers.
Responsive: scale 2516×1364 artboard to fit viewport proportionally.

Isometric Security Layers

Prompt 
Create a dramatic isometric illustration of four stacked floating
cream-colored rhombus/diamond layers on a near-black background.

── Canvas ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Background: #010201 (near-black)
• Viewport border: 4 px solid #bdb99d
• Aspect ratio: 2512 × 1360 px
• Responsive scaling to fit viewport

── Background Grid Lines ───────────────────────────────────────
Four horizontal dashed lines across the full width:
  y = 699, 889, 1080, 1271
  Color: #f4f0d9  Stroke: 6 px  Dash: 18 on / 16 off  Opacity: 0.92

── Four Isometric Layers (bottom to top) ───────────────────────
Cream-colored rhombus panels stacked vertically with ~190 px gaps.
All share:
  • Fill: #fffbed  (warm cream)
  • Stroke: #c8c6b5, 4 px
  • Drop shadow: dy=18, stdDeviation=18, flood-color black, opacity .35

Layer positions (polygon points for each):
  Bottom:  525,1273  1256,850  1987,1273  1660,1462  852,1462
  Layer 2: 525,1082  1256,659  1987,1082  1256,1318
  Layer 3: 525,891   1256,468  1987,891   1256,1127
  Top:     525,700   1256,274  1987,700   1256,1126

── Edge Nodes (diamond markers) ────────────────────────────────
At the left and right midpoints of each layer's horizontal datum,
place small diamond/rhombus markers (cream fill, colored stroke 4 px):
  Top layer (y≈699):    Purple  #8b20b5  — left & right
  Layer 3 (y≈890):      Green   #0e9b60  — left & right
  Layer 2 (y≈1081):     Amber   #f5a900  — left & right
  Bottom (y≈1272):      Blue    #2364ad  — left & right
Each diamond is ~17 px wide, 11 px tall.

── Top Surface Illustration ────────────────────────────────────
On the topmost layer surface, render a subtle abstract shape
resembling a stylized map or land mass:
  • Fill: #fff8d6, opacity 0.25
  • Stroke: #b9b7a5, 4 px
  • Two black filled shadow polygons overlapping it to suggest
    depth/terrain

── Visual Style ────────────────────────────────────────────────
High drama: dark background vs. luminous cream layers.
Dashed grid lines create a technical/blueprint feel.
Colored edge nodes mark "security levels" or data tiers.
Zero gradients except for the soft shadow filter.
Clean isometric geometry — no perspective distortion.

Floating Isometric Cards

Prompt 
Create a floating isometric card layout on a pale purple background,
with four large rhombus tiles each featuring a unique internal pattern.

── Canvas ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Background: #f4f2ff (soft lavender)
• Aspect ratio: 3200 × 2261 px
• Responsive scaling

── Shared Tile Anatomy ─────────────────────────────────────────
Each tile is a diamond (rhombus) with isometric depth sides:
  Top face:    fill #f6f3ff
  Left side:   fill #b8a8ff,  stroke #4b3bd8, 8 px, round joins
  Right side:  fill #a999f2,  stroke #4b3bd8, 8 px, round joins
  Top outline: stroke #4b3bd8, 8 px, round joins
  Drop shadow: dy=34, stdDeviation=26, color #5e4ddb, opacity 0.22
  Depth: 110–114 px

── Four Tiles & Their Patterns ─────────────────────────────────

Tile 1 — TOP CENTER  (x=1095, y=260, w=1420, h=660, depth=110)
  Pattern: LINE PATTERN
  ~21 horizontal parallel lines across the face, all clipped to tile.
  Lines run from left-center-ish to upper-right. Spacing is even.
  Top line: stroke #3a326c, 4.2 px, opacity 0.86
  Other lines: stroke #aaa4c7, 3.1 px, opacity 0.56, round caps

Tile 2 — LEFT MIDDLE  (x=245, y=730, w=1365, h=700, depth=112)
  Pattern: PULSE / ECG PATTERN
  Two jagged polylines resembling signal/heartbeat waveforms.
  Both lines: stroke #4d3fd6, 4 px, round linejoin
  Second line offset ~112 px left and ~70 px down from the first.

Tile 3 — RIGHT MIDDLE  (x=1785, y=805, w=1385, h=700, depth=112)
  Pattern: WAVE / ARC PATTERN
  ~18 concentric curved arcs (bezier curves) radiating outward.
  Stroke: #8e82df, 3 px
  Opacity fades from ~0.42 (inner) to ~0.08 (outer)

Tile 4 — BOTTOM CENTER  (x=948, y=1300, w=1370, h=705, depth=114)
  Pattern: DOT / SUNFLOWER PATTERN
  145 circles arranged in a Fibonacci/sunflower spiral (golden angle
  2.399 radians). Radii vary: 7–23 px.
  Primary dots: fill #a799ef, opacity 0.55
  Accent dots (every 5th): fill #7564dd, opacity 0.82

── Colour Palette ──────────────────────────────────────────────
Background   #f4f2ff    Tile top face  #f6f3ff
Left side    #b8a8ff    Right side     #a999f2
Outline      #4b3bd8    Shadow color   #5e4ddb
Line stroke  #aaa4c7    Pulse stroke   #4d3fd6
Wave stroke  #8e82df    Dot primary    #a799ef
Dot accent   #7564dd

── Visual Style ────────────────────────────────────────────────
Ethereal, geometric, data-visualization aesthetic.
Each tile tells a different data story through its pattern.
Tiles overlap slightly to create visual layering and depth.
No text, no labels — purely visual/abstract.
